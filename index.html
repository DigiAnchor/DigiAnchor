The Catalyst

A Regional Insight

The vision for DigiAnchor crystallized during founder Aditya Lingineni’s travels through rural Southeast Asia. While navigating these provinces, he observed a staggering disparity: brilliant, capable individuals were being systemically decoupled from the global economy due to a complete lack of digital infrastructure.
